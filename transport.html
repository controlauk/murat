How to Get to Yalova

By Ferry (from Istanbul)

From Yenikapı or Pendik

IDO (Istanbul Fast Ferries) offers high-speed ferry services directly to Yalova.
  • Ferry Duration: Approximately 1 hour.
  • Frequency: Ferries run frequently throughout the day.
  • Tickets: Can be purchased online or at the ferry terminal.

By bus

  • Yalova is well-connected by bus services from major cities in Turkey.
  • Bus companies like Kamil Koç, Metro Turizm, and Varan provide regular services to Yalova.
  • Bus Duration: Varies by departure city, typically ranging from 1.5 to 4 hours.
  • Tickets: Can be purchased online or at bus terminals.

By car

  • Yalova is accessible via the O-5 and O-6 highways.
  • From Istanbul, it takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours by car, depending on traffic.
  • Parking: Available in various locations around the city.

By train

  • Yalova does not have a direct train service, but you can take a train to nearby cities like Bursa or Izmit and then take a bus or taxi to Yalova.
  • Train Duration: Varies by route, typically 2 to 3 hours.
  • Tickets: Can be purchased online or at train stations.

By plane

  • The nearest airport is Sabiha Gökçen International Airport (SAW), located about 70 km from Yalova.
  • From the airport, you can take a shuttle bus or taxi to Yalova.
  • Flight Duration: Varies by departure city, typically 1 hour.
  • Tickets: Can be purchased online or at airline offices.
